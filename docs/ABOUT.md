# About TrinaxAI

## Why TrinaxAI Exists

TrinaxAI was born from a simple conviction: **AI should belong to everyone, not just big tech companies.**

In a world where AI assistants increasingly require cloud subscriptions, collect your data, and impose usage limits, TrinaxAI takes the opposite path. It is a **100% local, open-source AI assistant** that runs entirely on your machine. No cloud. No subscriptions. No limits.

The vision is clear — give developers, students, and everyday users the same powerful AI experience they'd get from commercial products, but with **privacy, freedom, and full control**. Your data never leaves your network. Your conversations are yours alone. Your models run on your hardware.

### Core Principles

- **Local-first** — Everything runs on your machine via Ollama. No API keys required.
- **Privacy-respecting** — Your code, your chats, your documents stay with you.
- **Open-source** — AGPL-3.0-or-later. The community can audit, fork, and improve.
- **Production-grade** — Not a toy project. Real features: RAG with hybrid search, voice mode, vision, PWA, CLI.
- **Bilingual by design** — Spanish and English, auto-detected. Built by a Latin American developer for a global audience.

TrinaxAI combines a ChatGPT-like chat interface (PWA) with a developer CLI, semantic code search with citations, voice conversations, and image analysis — all running locally with Ollama models.
